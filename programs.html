Our Programs & Services

We offer a variety of programs to engage the community in sustainable gardening practices.

Volunteer Days

Join us for regular volunteer days where we maintain the garden, plant new crops, and learn together.

  • Every Saturday: 9am-12pm
  • Wednesday evenings: 5pm-7pm (Seasonal)
  • Special seasonal events
Sign up for Volunteer Days

Workshops & Classes

  • Introduction to Urban Gardening
  • Composting 101
  • Seasonal Planting Guide
  • Container Gardening for Small Spaces
  • Preserving Your Harvest

Community Plots

We offer individual garden plots for community members to grow their own produce.

Plot rentals are available seasonally with guidance from our gardening experts.

School Programs

We partner with local schools to provide educational gardening experiences for students of all ages.

Harvest Sharing

Excess produce from our garden is shared with community members and local food banks.
