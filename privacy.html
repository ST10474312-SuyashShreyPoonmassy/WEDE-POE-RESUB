Payment Details Protection

Last Updated: May 6 2021

Our Commitment to Payment Security

At GreenSteps Community Garden, we are committed to safeguarding your payment details. This policy outlines the measures we take to ensure your financial information remains secure whenever you make transactions on our platform.

How We Protect Your Payment Information

Encryption Technology

At GreenSteps Community Garden, all payments are safeguarded through SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption. This technology ensures a protected link between your browser and our servers, keeping every transaction private and secure.

Tokenization

At GreenSteps Community Garden, we use tokenization to keep your payment details safe. This process replaces sensitive financial data with unique tokens that carry the necessary information for transactions, without exposing or compromising your security.

PCI DSS Compliance

GreenSteps Community Garden is fully compliant with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS). This means we strictly follow global standards when storing, processing, and transmitting credit or debit card information.

Secure Payment Gateways

We work with trusted payment processors that specialize in secure online transactions. Your payment details are never stored on our systems, but are handled directly by these certified providers.

What Payment Information We Collect

During checkout, we collect only necessary payment information, which may include:

  • Credit/Debit Card Type
  • Last 4 digits of your card number
  • Card expiration date
  • Billing address for verification

Important: We do not store your complete card number, CVV code, or PIN on our servers.

How We Use Your Payment Information

Your payment information is used exclusively for:

  • Processing your purchase transactions
  • Preventing fraudulent activities
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Providing refunds when applicable

Third-Party Payment Processors

We use trusted third-party payment processors including:

  • Master Card
  • PayPal
  • Other PCI-compliant payment providers

These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your information. We recommend reviewing their policies for complete understanding.

Your Role in Payment Security

You can help protect your payment information by:

  • Keeping your account password secure and confidential
  • Logging out after each session, especially on shared devices
  • Ensuring your device has updated antivirus and anti-malware protection
  • Checking for the padlock icon in your browser address bar during checkout
  • Regularly monitoring your bank and credit card statements

Data Retention

We retain minimal payment information only as long as necessary to:

  • Complete your transaction
  • Provide customer support related to the transaction
  • Comply with legal and accounting requirements
