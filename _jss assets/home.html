Welcome to GreenSteps

Join our community garden and grow together! We offer gardening plots, educational programs, and community events for all ages.

GreenSteps is a community space where people come together to grow fresh produce, share skills, and promote sustainable living. Our garden brings neighbours closer, provides affordable healthy food, and creates opportunities to learn about nature and eco-friendly practices.
