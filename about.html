Our Story

GreenSteps Community Garden was established in 2020 with a mission to create green spaces in urban environments and educate community members about sustainable gardening practices.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable living, foster community connections, and provide access to fresh produce through urban gardening education and hands-on experiences.

Our Values

  • Sustainability
  • Community Building
  • Education
  • Accessibility
  • Environmental Stewardship

Our Team

We are a group of dedicated volunteers, gardening experts, and community organizers committed to making our city greener and more sustainable.

Garden Location

Our main garden is located at 265 Effingham Road, Eco City, with additional satellite gardens throughout the community.
